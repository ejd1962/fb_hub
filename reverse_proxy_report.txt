REVERSE PROXY MAPPINGS
=====================

Proxy Port: 8999
Base URL: http://localhost:8999

Routes:
-------
[PROXY ] http://localhost:8999/localhost_10000              → localhost:10000 (backend  -- hub -- dev)
[PROXY ] http://localhost:8999/localhost_11000              → localhost:11000 (frontend -- hub -- dev-vite)
[PROXY ] http://localhost:8999/localhost_10001              → localhost:10001 (backend  -- app -- dev)
[PROXY ] http://localhost:8999/localhost_11001              → localhost:11001 (frontend -- app -- dev-vite)
