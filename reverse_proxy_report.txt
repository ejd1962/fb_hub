REVERSE PROXY MAPPINGS
=====================

Proxy Port: 8999
Base URL: http://localhost:8999

Routes:
-------
http://localhost:8999/localhost_10000              → localhost:10000 (hub)
http://localhost:8999/localhost_11000              → localhost:11000 (hub)
http://localhost:8999/localhost_10001              → localhost:10001 (dev)
http://localhost:8999/localhost_11001              → localhost:11001 (dev-vite)
